Bodybuilding: Hobby Site Midterm

This site was created as an assignment for one of my class midterms, but also made due to my love for bodybuilding as a sport. I initially wasn't too fond of bodybuilding when I started my fitness journey, but I quickly found myself more invested in the sport as time went on. I now follow a few competitions and influencers regulary, and actively engage in the hobby myself.

My Favorite Bodybuilders:

  • Tom Platz
  • Chris Bumstead
  • Noel Deyzel
  • Stephane Matala
  • Sam Sulek

Navigation Guide

A short summary of each page. This site will provide general information on bodybuilding, including a history of the sport, the science behind it, and my own personal habits.
  • About: A general summary of the sport, and a brief overview of the history.
  • Physiques: Physiques that I personally find appealing.
  • My-Routine: How I structure my personal bodybuilding training.
  • Resources: More resources on bodybuilding, because I don't know everything.
  • FAQ: A page to answer the frequently asked questions about bodybuilding and the site.

Why Bodybuilding?

Although bodybuilding can be as simple as going to the gym, training as hard as you can, and following healthy habits, there are plenty of intricacies and habits that people can build in order to complicate or simplify the hobby as much as one sees fit. My love for the hobby stems from the great community surrounding bodybuilding, and the health benefits it provides.
